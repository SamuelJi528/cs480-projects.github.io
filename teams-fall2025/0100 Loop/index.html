The Problem

  • Overpriced & limited parking near event venues.
  • Fans at arenas like Crypto, SoFi, YouTube Theater, and Intuit Dome often pay $70+.
  • Official lots sell out quickly, causing stress and late arrivals.
  • Residents have unused driveways/yards with no easy way to offer them.
  • Visitors face frustration; neighbors miss income opportunities.

Our Insight

There’s demand on both sides—event-goers want cheaper parking, residents want extra income.

The Solution

A web app connecting event-goers with nearby residents renting out driveways or yards.

  • Simple reservation with an interactive map
  • Affordable spots: $30 or less
  • Reduces parking stress and saves time
  • Creates a new income stream for locals
